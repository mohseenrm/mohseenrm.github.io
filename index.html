Passions

Design
Not particularly gifted in traditional arts (or anything in general), I started off doing graphic design for local events. After freelancing for a while, I gained a good sense of composition, effective color palletes, and an obsession with minor details. Nowadays, I dabble in vector design and animations. A lot of my recent designs take cues from magazines and large print mediums.
Photograph
I recently picked up photography, and continue learning and playing with different ways of capturing a picture. What I love most about photography is the process; composing the right frame, waiting for the subjects to get in position, the contrast in pictures and emotions, and post processing pictures for print and web.
Lift

I started strength training shortly after, and gravitated towards powerlifting. In my first powerlifting meet under USAPL (83 Kg), I got a 1,236 lbs total, which went better than my coach expected. I now am planning a comeback to ping pong along side my current strength training.
